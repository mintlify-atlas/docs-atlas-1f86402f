---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Get from login to your first sentiment analysis in under 5 minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SentimentAPI">
    Navigate to `/login` in your browser. Enter your **email address** and **password** (`contrasenia` field) and click **Iniciar Sesión**.

    On success, the server creates a JWT session stored in a secure `httpOnly` cookie and redirects you to the Analysis Panel at `/sentiment`. Your session remains active for **2 hours**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze a single comment">
    You are now on the Analysis Panel at `/sentiment`. Type any customer comment into the text input and click **Analizar Sentimientos**.

    The results table below the input shows one row per comment with three columns:

    | Column | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `texto` | The original comment you submitted |
    | `prevision` | A badge showing `POSITIVO` or `NEGATIVO` |
    | `probabilidad` | A progress bar representing the confidence score (0–1) |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Try bulk analysis">
    Navigate to `/sentiment/upload-csv`. Prepare a CSV file with at least a `texto` column — one comment per row:

    ```csv
    texto
    The delivery arrived on time and the product was exactly as described.
    Customer support was unresponsive and my issue was never resolved.
    Great quality, I will definitely buy again.
    ```

    Click **Seleccionar archivo**, choose your CSV, then click **Iniciar Procesamiento Masivo**. The platform processes every row through the AI model and displays a full results table with sentiment badges and probability bars.

    <Tip>CSV files must be under 3 MB. For larger datasets, split the file into multiple uploads.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your statistics">
    Navigate to `/sentiment/statistics`. Enter a number in the **Cantidad de registros** field to specify how many recent analyses you want to retrieve, then click **Analizar**.

    The page displays three summary cards at the top:

    - **Total records** — the number of analyses returned
    - **Positivos** — percentage of results classified as `POSITIVO`
    - **Negativos** — percentage of results classified as `NEGATIVO`

    Below the cards, a table lists each individual result with its `texto`, `prevision`, and `probabilidad`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export results">
    On any results table — single analysis, bulk analysis, or statistics — click **Exportar CSV**. Your browser downloads a formatted CSV file containing all the rows currently visible in the table.

    Use this file for reporting, archiving, or sharing results with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your session expires after 2 hours. If you see "No autenticado", navigate to `/login` and log in again to receive a fresh token.
</Note>
